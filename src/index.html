The Story

Where has everyone gone? This vibrant forest you call home has grown defensive; paths that were once open are now blocked to you and your kind…

You can’t get through this alone and, fortunately, you won’t have to! You play as a slime and you will come across many others of your kind on your journey. Team up with them to mimic the brightly colored flora of the forest and pass unimpeded. In this adventure puzzler, split, mix and match colors to overcome the challenges ahead!
